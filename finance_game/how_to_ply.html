Game Rules: Financial Master

Objective:

Grow your starting capital more than anyone else by making smart investment choices over several turns.

Starting Capital:

Each player begins with 1,000,000 Tenge.

Investment Options:

  • Stocks: Change range: +15% to -10%. Medium risk with potential for good returns.
  • Deposit: Change range: +10% to -10%. Low risk with small fluctuations in income.
  • Business: Change range: +50% to -70%. High risk, can result in big profits or big losses.
  • Realty (Real Estate): Change range: +2% to +10%. Low risk, stable returns, but slow growth.

Game Mechanics:

Players choose where to invest for the current turn. For each investment, a spinner determines a random percentage change. Changes depend on the investment ranges and random events, which can affect which investment grows or declines.

Winning:

After a set number of turns (e.g., 3 years in game time), the player with the highest capital increase wins. Strategic choices and careful planning are key to maximizing profits and minimizing losses.

Good Luck
